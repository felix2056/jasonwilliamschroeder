About Jason

Building leaders,
systems, and better jobsites.

Jason Schroeder is a lifelong builder, construction executive, and one of the industry's most trusted voices on Lean thinking, Takt Planning, production control, and field leadership.

With more than 30 years in construction, he has led work from field engineer to project director, built training systems across major firms, and founded Elevate Construction to help builders improve operations through remarkable training, project support, and consulting.

His mission is direct: make construction as efficient as manufacturing, as respected as healthcare, and better for the families who depend on it.
