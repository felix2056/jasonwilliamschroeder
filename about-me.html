About Jason

Founder of Elevate Construction, inventor of the Takt Production System®, and a lifelong builder focused on making construction more reliable, respected, and humane.

Jason William Schroeder

Jason Schroeder is a lifelong builder, construction executive, author, speaker, and one of the construction industry's most trusted voices on Lean thinking, Takt Planning, production control, and field leadership.

He is the founder and chief operating officer at Elevate Construction, where he helps construction professionals and companies improve their operations and performance through remarkable training, project support, and consulting.

His mission is to elevate the entire construction industry by building leaders, improving efficiency, and creating workplaces that respect and support employees. In Jason's words, the goal is to make construction as efficient as manufacturing and as respected as healthcare.

Professional Experiences

From field engineer to founder, Jason's path has been shaped by major project leadership, systems invention, and builder training at scale.

Elevate
Construction

Founder & Chief Operating Officer

Builder, podcast host, author, and speaker focused on improving construction industry operations through training, consulting, and project support.

Jason's work centers on operational performance, leadership training, project recovery, and operational transformation. He is also the creator of the Integrated Production Control System™ and inventor of the Takt Production System®. The Elevate Construction Podcast has surpassed one million downloads.

LeanBuilt
LLC

Chief Operating Officer & Co-Founder

A construction company created in partnership with Highstreet to demonstrate Lean construction methods in a real operating environment.

LeanBuilt serves as a training ground, research lab, and proof point for construction operations that value flow, predictability, and respect for workers and trade partners.

LeanTakt

Founder & Chief Operating Officer

A training and consulting platform dedicated to scaling Takt Planning and control throughout the construction industry.

Jason developed the Takt Guide, Takt Growth Boards, and trademark stewardship for the Takt Production System®. LeanTakt supports implementation, training, and consulting for teams that want a repeatable field system.

LeanSuperintendent

Founder & Chief Operating Officer

An educational platform built to train and support construction superintendents with the systems and resources they need in the field.

Jason authored Elevating Construction Superintendents and Elevating Construction Senior Superintendents, and developed tools such as the Superintendent Growth Board to help field leaders develop faster and lead more effectively.

Okland
Construction

Project Director / Field Operations Director

Led field operations including scheduling, surveying, and concrete departments on major projects.

Key accomplishments included directing the Creighton Health Science Campus, developing a 160-hour Field Engineer Boot Camp, creating a 210-hour Superintendent Boot Camp, leading preconstruction planning and logistics, and authoring The Art of The Builder.

DPR
Construction

Operations / General Superintendent / Project Superintendent

Oversaw field operations for major construction projects, with responsibility for production planning, scheduling, logistics, and construction execution.

Jason helped lead delivery on projects such as the Bioscience Research Laboratories Project and the Center for Gamma Ray Imaging, while also developing the Project Engineer Boot Camp training program.

Hensel
Phelps

Superintendent / Western District VDC Manager / Field Operations Trainer / Field Engineer

Held multiple leadership roles across field operations, VDC, training, and project delivery on large-scale projects.
